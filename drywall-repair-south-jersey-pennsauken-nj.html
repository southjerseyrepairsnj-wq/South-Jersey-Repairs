Pennsauken Drywall Repair FAQ

Do you handle row home drywall repairs in Pennsauken?

Yes. Row home drywall - including party walls, narrow hallways, and low-clearance stairwells - is common work for us in Pennsauken. We work in tight spaces and price the job based on scope, not the difficulty of access.

My ceiling has been patched multiple times and keeps cracking. What is the real fix?

Repeated cracking in the same area usually means the underlying tape has failed or the original plaster is shifting. Patching over it again won't hold. The right fix is to cut back to solid material, re-tape if needed, and apply compound in proper coats with sanding between each one. We do it that way the first time.

Can you handle drywall repair across multiple units in the same building?

Yes. If you own multiple units in a Pennsauken property - row homes, a duplex, or a small apartment building - we can schedule the work together and price it as a package.
