Turnersville Drywall Repair FAQ

Do the hairline cracks above my doors in Turnersville need to be fixed before painting?

Yes. Hairline cracks above doorways are caused by the door frame moving slightly as the house settles and seasonal humidity causes wood to expand and contract. If you paint over them, they reopen through the new paint within a season or two. We fill and tape these cracks properly so they hold under paint rather than reopening.
