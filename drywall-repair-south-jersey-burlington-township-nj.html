Burlington Township Drywall Repair FAQ

I have nail pops all across my ceiling in Burlington Township. Do I need to fix them all at once?

Not necessarily. We can do a full ceiling nail pop repair in one visit - setting screws, filling both the new screw dimples and the original pop locations, sanding, and priming. Or we can address a specific room. It is more efficient to do all the nail pops in a room at once since the compound, sanding, and priming process is the same regardless of the count.
