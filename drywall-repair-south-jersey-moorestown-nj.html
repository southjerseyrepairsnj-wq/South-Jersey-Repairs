Moorestown Drywall Repair FAQ

What drywall issues are most common in older Moorestown homes?

Settling cracks above door frames and along ceiling seams are the most common issues in older Moorestown colonials and capes. These happen gradually as the house settles over decades. We cut out the loose tape or compound, apply new mesh or paper tape, and re-coat so the repair holds long term rather than cracking again in a year.

Do you work with real estate agents and sellers in Moorestown?

Yes. A significant portion of our Moorestown work is seller prep - fixing the wall and ceiling issues that show up on buyer walkthroughs or inspection reports. We can turn around most drywall punch lists quickly so listings stay on schedule.

Can you match the existing texture on my walls?

Smooth walls are the most common finish in Moorestown homes, which makes blending easier. If your walls have a light orange peel or knockdown texture, we test the spray or trowel pattern on scrap first before touching the repair area.
