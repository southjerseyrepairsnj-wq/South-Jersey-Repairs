Seasonal Home Maintenance Checklist

Small seasonal checks help prevent leaks, drafts, safety issues, and bigger repair costs.

Spring

  • Check exterior caulk, fence damage, decks, railings, and pressure washing needs.
  • Look for winter water stains, drywall cracks, and gutter overflow marks.
  • Plan fence repairs, exterior touch-ups, and cleanup projects.

Summer

  • Handle deck staining, pressure washing, outdoor repairs, and camera installs.
  • Check doors, trim, windows, and screens during heavy use.
  • Fix small exterior issues before fall weather arrives.

Fall & Winter

  • Seal drafts, repair door issues, and check interior safety items.
  • Handle drywall, fixture, bathroom, and indoor punch list work.
  • Prepare rental or move-out repairs before holiday schedules fill up.
Tip: If you wait until everything is urgent, repairs cost more and scheduling gets harder. A seasonal punch list keeps the work manageable.
